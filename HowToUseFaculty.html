How To Use: Faculty

Entering Information

In order to enter information into the WheresMyProf database, you'll need to utilize
the Professor Office Hours form to input your office hours as well as the Professor Class
Form to input the exact time you are teaching your classes.


Office Hours Form

Inside of the Professor Office Hours form you will first be prompted to enter your building
location as well as your room number. The next step is to click on the bubbles that correspond
to the days on which your office hours will be occurring. You will then need to input the times
at which your office hours will be occurring on the days you inputted previously. Leave any days
where you don't have scheduled office hours blank. You can then submit the form.


Class Hours Form

The times you are hosting classes will be entered utilizing the Professor and Course Form.
To start off with the user is prompted to enter both the first and last name of the specified
professor. You will then be asked to enter your office building and room number. Next you will
be asked to enter what department your class is in as well as the class name and course number.
Next you will be asked to input what days the class occurs as well as the start and end time for
each day. If you don't have class on a specific day leave the start time and end time for that day
blank.
