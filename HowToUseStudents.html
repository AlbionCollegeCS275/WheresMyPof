How To Use: Students

Getting Information

In order to to get professor information as a student you will need to go through
the query page. Once you are at the query page, you will see Search by Department in
big bold letters. Underneath Search by Department, you will see a drop down menu. Inside
of this drop down menu is a list of all of the departments offered by Albion College. Select
the desired department that your professor is from and a list of professors will then be displayed.
Select the desired professor from the list of professors in the specified department to view your
professor's information.
