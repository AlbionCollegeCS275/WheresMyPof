Project Overview

Short Description:

WheresMyProf uses crowdsourced information to provide both professors and students an easy way to share schedule
information. This project utilizes multiple different scripting languages to help accomplish this task.


Project Rationale:

This project was created as a way to make the lives of both students and faculty at Albion College easier. Before
the creation of WheresMyProf, students at Albion College often struggled with when exactly their professors were free
to meet them about school conflicts and more. Professors also often struggled with effectively getting their schedule
informaton out to students. The WheresMyProf application was created to help fix these scheduling issues.


Comaptible Hardware:

WheresMyProf is currently only compatible with desktop computers and laptops at this time. We hope to eventually
be able to push this product out as an application for smart phones.
